Who We are?

GP Aarogya Healthcare Technology Private Limited is Asia's fastest-growing technology-driven affordable healthcare company, dedicated to
revolutionizing the healthcare landscape in India. We proudly stand as the nation’s first organization to offer OPD card facilities tailored for both
individuals and their family members, making quality healthcare more accessible than ever before.

At GP Aarogya Healthcare, our mission transcends traditional healthcare delivery. We are committed to transforming the fragmented, sectoral approach of health service delivery into a comprehensive, need-based model that prioritizes the holistic well-being of our clients. Our innovative schemes are designed to implement groundbreaking interventions that effectively address the healthcare system at all levels—primary, secondary, and tertiary—encompassing prevention, promotion, and ambulatory care.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, and we are dedicated to empowering individuals and families to lead healthier, happier lives. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and a compassionate approach, we aim to create a healthcare ecosystem that fosters well-being, enhances quality of life, and promotes sustainable health practices.

Join us on our journey to redefine healthcare and make a meaningful impact in the lives of millions.

OUR PRODUCT

AAROGYA KAVACH

NAYE BHARAT KA SURAKSHA KAVACH

Aarogya Kavach is your trusted partner in health protection, designed to provide you with peace of mind in
uncertain times. This comprehensive health plan acts as a shield against unexpected health risks, ensuring
that you and your loved ones receive quality outpatient care when needed.

The Aarogya Kavach policy includes essential details that are vital for accessing your benefits.
This information encompasses the insured person's name, date of birth, policy serial number, and
the sum assured.Each of these components is crucial for ensuring you can take full advantage of
the OPD services available to you.

Having a reliable health plan is not just a necessity; it is an essential part of securing your family's future.
With Aarogya Kavach, you can rest assured that you have taken a proactive step towards safeguarding your
health and financial well-being through accessible outpatient facilities.
