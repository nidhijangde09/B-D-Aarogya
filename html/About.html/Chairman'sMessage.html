CEO Message

“Saving lives and contributing to a healthier india a GP Aarogya!”

An autorickshaw driver doesn't earn much but has a big family to feed. Health often gets a second priority, sometimes with debilitating consequences.

Sometimes these drivers don't realise that they're suffering from a major ailment until it's too late. llliteracy and lack of awareness are major factors, not to mention omnipresent hiatus of access and affordability.

"We in GP Aarogya (Aarogya Kavach) realise this and have taken the ownership of their health through our low cost disruptive preventive health and wellness model. More then 400 autorickshaw drivers nad their families in delhi NCR are being covered."

Our teams often reaches out to various auto assembly points and help onboard them on our platform in between their rides and otherwise a very busy schedule.

Enthused by our excellent services, most of them have now started enrolling their friends and extended family member's too, benefiting even more people.

Sometimes a journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. We're just beginning

Just another productive day at GP Aarogya (Aarogya Kavach), saving lives and ensuring better health for the underserved Masses!
